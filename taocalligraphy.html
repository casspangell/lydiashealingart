How does the Tao Calligraphy Healing happen?

Tao Calligraphy is a revolutionary healing art based on the ancient art of Yi Bi Zi calligraphy – a cursive (Oneness) writing that Master Sha learned from sole lineage holder, Professor Li Qiu Yun. At over 100 years of age, Professor Li passed the lineage to Master Sha. Given Master Sha’s life-long training and mastery of ancient philosophies, healing arts, spiritual practices and more, he was guided in a sacred way to profoundly connect with Tao Oneness. When Master Sha writes Yi Bi Zi style, he connects with Tao Oneness and Tao Source pours its essence (information, energy, and matter) into the calligraphy, transforming the Yi Bi Zi writing into a Tao Calligraphy that radiates the pure, positive information, energy and matter of Tao love, forgiveness, harmony, flourishing and more.

This positive, high-frequency field is within the Tao Calligraphy. We can absorb the high frequencies, and let go of what blocks us from greater health, happiness, and success. With targeted practice with Tao Calligraphy, we can reach new heights spiritually, physically, mentally, and emotionally. We can accumulate more positive information energy and matter which translates into positive changes in our lives.

Master Sha teaches the Six Tao Healing Techniques (Body Power, Mind Power, Sound Power, Soul Power, Breathing Power, and Tao Calligraphy Power) so that people around the world can learn how to receive maximum benefit from the Tao Calligraphy to lead happier, healthier lives.
